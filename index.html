My name is Manja Wilson. I'm 33 and I've been a stay-at-home mom for the last 7 years.

Background

I was born and raised in Germany, where I graduated from High School in 2008, and then came to the US to be an Au Pair.
My time as an Au Pair wasn't always easy, but it prepared me in a lot of ways for being a mom myself. It also gave me the opportunity to travel a little within the US.
Between 2009 and 2010 I visited the Poconos (PA), Atlanta, Virginia, San Francisco, Miami, Myrtle Beach, and Hawaii.
While I was still an Au Pair, I met my husband, Joe, and after my Au Pair time was over, I stayed with him and we got married in 2011.
Over the years I worked in different retail jobs and eventually as a bank teller.

Me & Coding*

I have absolutely no experience with coding. I consider myself to posess about average computer skills:

  • researching things online
  • online shopping
  • the occasional word document or excel sheet
But I like to think I am a quick study and I am always willing and excited to learn new skills.
I have a friend who works from home doing what I now am sure is considered data analysis.
Over the years I tried to get a job at the same company a couple of times, but have never gotten anywhere.
A job from home would be ideal for me, being that I have to be home for my daughter before and after school, when she is sick,
and during the summer and other school breaks.
Then I recently heard of someone attending a coding bootcamp and then getting a job working from home.
They recommended Coding Temple, so I did some research. Finding out I could learn a lot in a fairly short amount of time,
and not being $30,000, $40,000 or even more in debt afterwards, I was sold.
I already have a feeling that this was a great decision. I haven't even started classes yet, and I feel that I have already learned a ton!
I mean, look at me creating this website!!

*Please be aware that at the time this website was created, my bootcamp hadn't started yet.
